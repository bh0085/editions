story

Nami Moni is created by ADAM O'DAY ANDREW JACOB AERONAUT JAPAS in celebration of great beer inspired by delicious art.

"Wave Things" in Japanese, we created this artwork in consideration of the connectedness of our nations, the joys of international collaboration and the celebration of friendship.

artistic statement

NAMI MONO was a collaboration between JAPAS, AERONAUT, ANDREW JACOB and ADAM O'DAY. The beer was envisioned by our brew teams to explore the unique flavor possibilities of our countries and our experiences. The art is a celebration of the connectedness of our countries, our communities, and our cultures. Our first release in the AERONAUT Editions series, this vivid graphic features four layers of vivid artwork created by Adam and Andy, inspired by treasures of the sea discovered in Provincetown MA which may have traveled from far away places.
