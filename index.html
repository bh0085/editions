Nami mono is created by ADAM O'DAY ANDREW JACOB AERONAUT JAPAS in celebration of great beer inspired by delicious art. "Wave Things" in Japanese, we created this artwork in consideration of the connectedness of our nations, the joys of international collaboration and the celebration of friendship. Nami Mono is the first release in our series of artwork-inspired beers, AERONAUT editions and is available in a limited run of 50 limited-edition lithographs on archival artistic paper by Funk & Schuster in Provincetown Massachusetts.
